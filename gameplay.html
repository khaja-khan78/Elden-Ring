The Chapel of Anticipation

Before exploring the Lands Between, you start by creating your character—choosing a class and keepsake item that shape your early game. After customizing, a cutscene introduces you inside the Chapel of Anticipation.

Loot the glowing body to get the Tarnished’s Wizened Finger. Cross the bridge and a boss, the Grafted Scion, will ambush you. Defeating it gives rare loot, but losing still progresses the story as you awaken elsewhere.

Stranded Graveyard & Tutorial

After awakening, you’ll get healing flasks and meet a ghost near a pit. Jump in to access the tutorial area. It teaches basic combat like dodging, blocking, and using magic or melee attacks.

You’ll face the Soldier of Godrick at the end—a simple boss meant to help you practice. Beating him gives Runes and the “Strength!” gesture.

Moving Forward

After the tutorial, collect multiplayer items and activate a nearby Site of Grace. You'll see an Imp Statue, which needs two Stonesword Keys to open a hidden dungeon. It's tough, so consider returning later.

Ride the lift, climb the stairs, and open the door to reach Limgrave—the start of your true journey in Elden Ring.
