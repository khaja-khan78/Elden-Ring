Welcome to the Elden Ring Fan Site

Dive into the hauntingly beautiful world of The Lands Between, where power, mystery, and ruin intertwine. FromSoftware's latest masterpiece, Elden Ring, brings a new dimension to the action RPG genre, blending rich lore with open-world exploration and brutal combat.

This fan site is your gateway to exploring key aspects of the game—from its intricate lore and legendary bosses to its atmospheric gameplay and critical acclaim. Whether you're a newcomer or a seasoned Tarnished, there's something here for every fan of Elden Ring.

Creators Behind Elden Ring

Hidetaka Miyazaki, the president of FromSoftware and visionary behind the Souls series, served as the game director of Elden Ring. His unique design philosophy emphasizes exploration, mystery, and challenge, shaping the game’s unforgettable world and mechanics.

George R.R. Martin, best known for his epic fantasy series A Song of Ice and Fire (adapted into HBO’s Game of Thrones), was brought on to create the deep and mythic lore of the Elden Ring universe. His contributions laid the foundation for the game’s setting and ancient history.
