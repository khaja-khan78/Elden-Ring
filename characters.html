Bosses

Malenia, Blade of Miquella(also known as Malenia, Goddess of Rot) is a two-phase Demigod Boss. She is the Empyrean twin sister of Miquella and is famous for her legendary battle against Starscourge Radahn, where she unleashed the Scarlet Rot and reduced Caelid to ruins. Defeating her is optional, but she is among the most iconic bosses in the game.

“I am Malenia, Blade of Miquella the unyielding force that never falters, even in the face of the Scarlet Rot.”

Starscourge Radahn is a Legend Boss and one of the mightiest living demigods. He fought Malenia to a standstill during the Shattering, but was driven mad by the Scarlet Rot. Radahn is located in Caelid and accessed via Redmane Castle during the Radahn Festival.

“The Red Lion General wielded gravitational powers which he learned in Sellia during his younger days. All so he would never have to abandon his beloved but scrawny steed.”

Morgott, the Omen King is the true identity of Margit, the Fell Omen, who confronts the Tarnished early in their journey. He is a child of Queen Marika and Godfrey, born cursed as an Omen and hidden beneath Leyndell, the Royal Capital. Despite being shunned by society, Morgott remains loyal to the Golden Order, acting as its last true guardian. He secretly defends the Erdtree from all who seek to claim its power, including the Tarnished. When confronted in the Capital, he reveals his identity and fights with great ferocity, wielding cursed blood and spectral weapons. Though burdened by rejection and inner torment, Morgott remains a tragic figure—loyal to a world that never accepted him.

“None may claim the title of Elden Lord… without facing me.”

Godrick the Grafted is a distant descendant of Queen Marika and Godfrey, though his divine blood is diluted. After the Elden Ring was shattered, he fled Leyndell during the Shattering and settled in Stormveil Castle in Limgrave. There, he began the grotesque practice of grafting limbs onto himself and others to gain power.Godrick was known for his arrogance and obsession with legacy, idolizing his ancestor Godfrey and calling himself "lord of all that is golden," though he was widely mocked and feared. When the Tarnished arrives, they discover horrifying signs of his grafting experiments. In battle, Godrick cuts off his own hand and grafts a dragon's head to it, unleashing fire. Despite this, he is defeated and his Great Rune claimed.Later, Nepheli Loux is named ruler of Limgrave, restoring dignity to the region.

“Witness the power of the Golden Lineage!”

Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy is a demigod and the head of Volcano Manor. Once a noble son of Queen Rennala and Radagon, Rykard rejected the Golden Order and sought immortality through unholy means. In his quest for power, he fused with the God-Devouring Serpent, sacrificing his humanity to become a monstrous being. From his fortress on Mt. Gelmir, he led a rebellion against the Erdtree, inviting Tarnished to join his cause of defiance. Rykard embodies chaos and ambition, consumed by his desire to devour the gods themselves.

“Join the Serpent King, as family…”

Mohg, Lord of Blood is a demigod shunned for his accursed Omen birth. Born of Queen Marika and Godfrey, Mohg was imprisoned deep beneath Leyndell, but he embraced the darkness within. Devoting himself to the mysterious Formless Mother, a deity of blood, he built the underground dynasty of Mohgwyn Palace, seeking to establish a new order ruled by blood and ambition. Mohg abducted Miquella, the most beloved of Empyreans, hoping to elevate him as a god and rule beside him. His twisted dreams culminate in a brutal battle beneath a blood-red sky.

“Miquella is mine, and mine alone.”
